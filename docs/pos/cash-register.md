# Cash Register

Cash registers track your POS terminal's cash flow. Each session records the opening balance, sales, and closing balance.

## Setting Up a Register

1. Navigate to **Settings → Cash Registers**
2. Click **+ New Register**
3. Enter the register name and location
4. Click **Save**

## Opening a Session

1. Navigate to **POS**
2. Select your register
3. Enter the **Opening Cash Balance**
4. Click **Open Session**

## Closing a Session

1. Click **Close Session** in the POS screen
2. Enter the **Counted Cash** amount
3. Ballie calculates the difference (over/short)
4. Click **Close & Save**
