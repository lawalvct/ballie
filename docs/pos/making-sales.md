# Making Sales

The POS checkout screen lets you quickly process sales, apply discounts, and accept payments.

## Processing a Sale

1. Open your active POS session
2. Add items by searching or scanning barcodes
3. Adjust quantities as needed
4. Apply discounts (per item or whole order)
5. Click **Charge** to proceed to payment

## Payment Options

- **Cash** — Enter amount tendered, Ballie calculates change
- **Card** — Record card payment
- **Bank Transfer** — Record transfer reference
- **Split Payment** — Combine multiple methods on one sale

## Receipts

After payment:
- **Print Receipt** — Send to connected receipt printer
- **Email Receipt** — Send to customer's email
- **Download PDF** — Save a digital copy
