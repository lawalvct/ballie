# Vendors

Track your suppliers and service providers. Vendor records link to purchase orders, expenses, and payments.

## Adding a Vendor

1. Navigate to **CRM → Vendors**
2. Click **+ New Vendor**
3. Fill in vendor details (name, contact, address, tax ID)
4. Click **Save**
