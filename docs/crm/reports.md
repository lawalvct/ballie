# CRM Reports

Analyze your customer and vendor relationships with built-in reports.

## Available Reports

- **Customer Aging** — Outstanding invoices grouped by age (30, 60, 90+ days)
- **Vendor Aging** — Outstanding payables by vendor
- **Customer Sales** — Sales totals per customer
- **Vendor Purchases** — Purchase totals per vendor

## Accessing Reports

1. Navigate to **CRM → Reports** or **Reports → Sales/Purchase Reports**
2. Select the report type
3. Set the date range and filters
4. Click **Generate**
