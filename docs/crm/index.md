# CRM

Ballie's CRM module helps you manage your customer and vendor relationships. Track contacts, record activities, import records in bulk, and generate aging reports.

## Features

- **Customers** — Full customer management with import, opening balances, and activity tracking
- **Vendors** — Vendor records for procurement and expense tracking
- **CRM Reports** — Customer and vendor aging analysis

## Quick Start

1. [Add your customers](/crm/customers) manually or via bulk import
2. [Add your vendors](/crm/vendors)
3. View [CRM reports](/crm/reports) for aging and activity analysis
