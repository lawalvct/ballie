# Procurement

Manage your purchasing process with purchase orders that integrate with inventory and accounting.

## Features

- **Purchase Orders** — Create, approve, and receive purchase orders from vendors

## Quick Start

1. Add your [vendors](/crm/vendors) in the CRM module
2. [Create purchase orders](/procurement/purchase-orders)
3. Receive goods and update inventory automatically
