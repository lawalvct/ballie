# Purchase Orders

Create purchase orders to formalize your procurement process. Track orders from creation through approval to receipt.

## Creating a Purchase Order

1. Navigate to **Procurement → Purchase Orders**
2. Click **+ New Purchase Order**
3. Fill in:
   - **Vendor** — Select from your vendor list
   - **Order Date** and **Expected Delivery Date**
   - **Items** — Add products with quantities and rates
4. Click **Save**

## Purchase Order Workflow

1. **Draft** — Created but not yet approved
2. **Approved** — Ready to send to vendor
3. **Sent** — Shared with the vendor
4. **Partially Received** — Some items received
5. **Received** — All items received, inventory updated
6. **Cancelled** — Order cancelled

## Receiving Goods

When goods arrive:
1. Open the purchase order
2. Click **Receive Items**
3. Enter the quantities received
4. Inventory is updated automatically
