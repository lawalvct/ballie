# Stock Management

Track every stock movement in your business — from purchases and sales to adjustments and transfers.

## Stock Journals

Stock journals record manual adjustments to inventory:

1. Navigate to **Inventory → Stock Journals**
2. Click **+ New Journal**
3. Select the **Journal Type** (Stock In, Stock Out, Adjustment)
4. Add the products and quantities
5. Enter a **Reason/Narration**
6. Click **Save**

## Stock Movements

Every stock change is recorded automatically:
- **Sales** — Reduces stock when an invoice or POS sale is made
- **Purchases** — Increases stock when purchase orders are received
- **Adjustments** — Manual corrections via stock journals
- **Transfers** — Move stock between locations
