# Categories & Units

Organize your products with categories and define units of measurement for accurate stock tracking.

## Product Categories

1. Navigate to **Inventory → Categories**
2. Click **+ New Category**
3. Enter the **Category Name** and optional description
4. Click **Save**

## Units of Measurement

Define how products are measured and sold:
- Pieces (pcs)
- Kilograms (kg)
- Litres (ltr)
- Cartons
- Custom units
