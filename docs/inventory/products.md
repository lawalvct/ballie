# Products

Manage your product catalog with detailed information including pricing, images, SKU codes, and stock levels.

## Adding a Product

1. Navigate to **Inventory → Products**
2. Click **+ New Product**
3. Fill in:
   - **Product Name** — The display name
   - **SKU** — Unique stock-keeping unit code
   - **Category** — Select a product category
   - **Unit** — Unit of measurement (pieces, kg, etc.)
   - **Selling Price** — Customer-facing price
   - **Cost Price** — Your purchase/manufacturing cost
   - **Opening Stock** — Initial quantity on hand
   - **Product Image** (optional)
4. Click **Save**

## Managing Products

- **Edit** — Update product details at any time
- **View Stock** — See current stock level and movement history
- **Disable** — Remove from active listings without deleting
