# Leave Management

Handle employee leave requests with configurable leave types, approval workflows, and automatic balance tracking.

## Leave Types

Configure leave types such as:
- Annual Leave
- Sick Leave
- Maternity/Paternity Leave
- Compassionate Leave
- Custom types

## Requesting Leave

1. Navigate to **Payroll → Leaves**
2. Click **+ New Leave Request**
3. Select the **Leave Type**, **Start Date**, and **End Date**
4. Add optional notes
5. Click **Submit**

## Approving Leave

Managers can approve or reject leave requests:
1. View pending requests in the Leaves section
2. Click **Approve** or **Reject**
3. Employee is notified of the decision
