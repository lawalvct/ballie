# Departments & Positions

Organize your company structure with departments and positions.

## Managing Departments

1. Navigate to **Payroll → Departments**
2. Click **+ New Department**
3. Enter the department name and optional head of department
4. Click **Save**

## Managing Positions

1. Navigate to **Payroll → Positions**
2. Click **+ New Position**
3. Enter the position title and assign it to a department
4. Click **Save**
