# Running Payroll

Process payroll for your employees with automatic calculations for earnings, deductions, statutory contributions, and net pay.

## Payroll Processing Steps

1. Navigate to **Payroll → Processing**
2. Click **+ New Payroll Run**
3. Select the **Pay Period** (monthly, bi-weekly)
4. Review the payroll summary:
   - Gross earnings per employee
   - Deductions (pension, tax, loans, etc.)
   - Net pay
5. Make any adjustments if needed
6. Click **Process Payroll**

## Payslips

After processing:
- Individual payslips are generated for each employee
- Download payslips as PDF
- Email payslips directly to employees
- Export bank payment files for bulk transfers

## Reports

- **Payroll Summary** — Overview of all payroll costs
- **Bank Schedule** — Payment file for bank transfers
- **Tax Report** — PAYE deductions summary
- **Detailed Report** — Line-by-line breakdown per employee
