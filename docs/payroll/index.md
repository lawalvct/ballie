# Payroll & HR

Ballie's Payroll & HR module manages your entire workforce lifecycle — from onboarding employees to processing payroll with Nigerian statutory compliance.

## Features

- **Employees** — Complete employee profiles with documents and onboarding
- **Departments & Positions** — Organizational structure and hierarchy
- **Salary Components** — Earnings, deductions, allowances, and tax brackets
- **Attendance & Shifts** — QR code check-in/out, shift schedules, overtime
- **Leave Management** — Leave types, requests, approvals, and balance tracking
- **Loans & Advances** — Employee loan management with repayment schedules
- **Payroll Processing** — Run payroll, generate payslips, manage pay periods
- **Statutory Compliance** — PAYE tax, pension (PFA), NHF deductions
