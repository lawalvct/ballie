# Statutory Compliance

Ballie handles Nigerian statutory deductions automatically — PAYE income tax, pension contributions, and NHF.

## PAYE (Pay-As-You-Earn)

Ballie calculates PAYE tax based on Nigeria's graduated tax bands:
- Automatically applied to taxable income
- Tax relief and allowances are factored in
- Monthly tax deductions included in payslips

## Pension

- **Employee Contribution** — Typically 8% of basic + housing + transport
- **Employer Contribution** — Typically 10%
- PFA (Pension Fund Administrator) details stored per employee
- Pension schedules generated for remittance

## NHF (National Housing Fund)

- 2.5% of basic salary
- Applied to eligible employees
- Included in payroll deductions

## Configuration

Navigate to **Payroll → Settings** or **Statutory → Settings** to configure:
- Tax brackets and relief amounts
- Pension rates and PFA details
- NHF applicability
