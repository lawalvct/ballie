# Attendance & Shifts

Track employee attendance using QR code check-in/out, manage shift schedules, and calculate overtime.

## QR Code Attendance

1. Employees scan a QR code to check in and out
2. Ballie records the timestamp and calculates hours worked
3. Late arrivals and early departures are flagged automatically

## Shift Schedules

1. Navigate to **Payroll → Shifts**
2. Create shift templates (e.g., Morning 8am–4pm, Evening 4pm–12am)
3. Assign employees to shifts

## Overtime Tracking

- Overtime is calculated based on hours worked beyond the scheduled shift
- Configure overtime rules and rates in payroll settings
- Overtime records can be approved before payroll processing
