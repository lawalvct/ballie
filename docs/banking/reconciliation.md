# Bank Reconciliation

Reconciliation ensures your Ballie records match your actual bank statements. Do this regularly to catch discrepancies.

## Reconciliation Process

1. Navigate to **Banking → Reconciliation**
2. Select the **Bank Account** and **Statement Date**
3. Enter the **Closing Balance** from your bank statement
4. Match transactions:
   - Ballie shows unreconciled transactions
   - Check off items that appear on your bank statement
5. Review the reconciliation summary
6. Click **Complete Reconciliation**

::: tip
Reconcile at least monthly to keep your records accurate and catch any discrepancies early.
:::
