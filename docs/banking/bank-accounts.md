# Bank Accounts

Add your business bank accounts to track cash flow and enable reconciliation.

## Adding a Bank Account

1. Navigate to **Banking → Bank Accounts**
2. Click **+ New Bank Account**
3. Enter:
   - **Bank Name** — Select from the list of Nigerian banks
   - **Account Name** — Your account name at the bank
   - **Account Number** — Your bank account number
   - **Opening Balance** — Starting balance for the account
4. Click **Save**

## Managing Accounts

- View the current balance and recent transactions
- Edit account details as needed
- Disable accounts that are no longer in use
