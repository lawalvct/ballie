# Banking

Manage your business bank accounts and reconcile transactions to keep your books accurate.

## Features

- **Bank Accounts** — Add and manage multiple bank accounts with opening balances
- **Reconciliation** — Match bank statements with your accounting records

## Quick Start

1. [Add your bank accounts](/banking/bank-accounts)
2. Record transactions through invoices, expenses, and payments
3. [Reconcile](/banking/reconciliation) periodically with your bank statements
