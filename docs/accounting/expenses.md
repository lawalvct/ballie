# Expenses

Track your business spending by recording expenses in Ballie. Each expense is linked to a ledger account and can be categorized for reporting.

## Recording an Expense

1. Navigate to **Accounting → Expenses**
2. Click **+ New Expense**
3. Fill in:
   - **Expense Category** — Select or create a category
   - **Amount** — The expense value
   - **Date** — When the expense occurred
   - **Vendor** (optional) — Who you paid
   - **Description** — Details about the expense
4. Click **Save**
