# Prepaid Expenses

Prepaid expenses allow you to record payments made in advance (e.g., annual rent, insurance premiums) and spread the cost over the relevant periods through automatic amortization.

## Setting Up a Prepaid Expense

1. Navigate to **Accounting → Prepaid Expenses**
2. Click **+ New Prepaid Expense**
3. Enter the details:
   - **Description** — What the prepaid expense is for
   - **Total Amount** — The full prepaid amount
   - **Start Date** — When amortization begins
   - **Number of Periods** — How many months to spread the cost
   - **Expense Account** — Where the monthly expense posts
4. Click **Save**

## Amortization Schedule

Ballie automatically generates a monthly amortization schedule. Each period, the proportional amount is expensed from the prepaid asset account to the designated expense account.
