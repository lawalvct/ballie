# Payments

Manage all incoming and outgoing payments. Payments can be recorded against invoices, expenses, or as standalone transactions.

## Recording a Payment

1. Navigate to **Accounting → Payments**
2. Click **+ New Payment**
3. Select the payment direction (received or made)
4. Choose the **Customer/Vendor** and link to an invoice if applicable
5. Enter the **Amount** and **Payment Method**
6. Click **Save**

## Payment Methods

Ballie supports multiple payment methods:
- Cash
- Bank Transfer
- Card Payment
- Mobile Money
- Cheque

## Bulk Payment Upload

For processing multiple payments at once, use the bulk upload feature:
1. Download the payment template
2. Fill in payment details
3. Upload the completed file
4. Review and confirm
