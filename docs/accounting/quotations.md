# Quotations & Proforma

Create quotations (proforma invoices) for your customers before committing to a full invoice. When accepted, convert them to invoices with one click.

## Creating a Quotation

1. Navigate to **Accounting → Quotations**
2. Click **+ New Quotation**
3. Select the **Customer** and add line items
4. Set a **Valid Until** date
5. Click **Save**

## Converting to Invoice

1. Open an approved quotation
2. Click **Convert to Invoice**
3. Review the pre-filled invoice details
4. Click **Save** to create the invoice
