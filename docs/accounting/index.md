# Accounting

Ballie's Accounting module provides a complete double-entry bookkeeping system designed for Nigerian businesses. Every transaction is automatically recorded with proper debit and credit entries, keeping your books balanced at all times.

## Features

- **Chart of Accounts** — Pre-configured account groups and ledger accounts with full customization
- **Invoices** — Create, send, and track customer invoices with automatic ledger posting
- **Quotations & Proforma** — Generate quotations and convert them to invoices in one click
- **Vouchers** — Journal vouchers for manual adjustments, with post/unpost capability
- **Expenses** — Record and categorize business expenses
- **Payments** — Track payments received and made, including bulk payment uploads
- **Prepaid Expenses** — Set up prepaid expenses with automatic amortization schedules

## Quick Start

1. Ensure your [Chart of Accounts](/accounting/chart-of-accounts) is configured (done during onboarding)
2. Add your [Customers](/crm/customers) and [Vendors](/crm/vendors) in the CRM module
3. Start [creating invoices](/accounting/invoices) for your sales
4. [Record expenses](/accounting/expenses) as they occur
5. View your [financial reports](/reports/financial-reports) to track business performance
