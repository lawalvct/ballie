# Support Tickets

Submit support tickets when you need help with Ballie. Track your requests and communicate with the support team.

## Creating a Ticket

1. Navigate to **Support** from the sidebar
2. Click **+ New Ticket**
3. Fill in:
   - **Subject** — Brief description of your issue
   - **Category** — Select the relevant area (Billing, Technical, Feature Request)
   - **Priority** — Low, Medium, High
   - **Description** — Detailed explanation of your issue
   - **Attachments** — Upload screenshots or files (optional)
4. Click **Submit**

## Tracking Tickets

- View all your tickets with status indicators
- Add replies and additional information
- Receive email notifications on updates
