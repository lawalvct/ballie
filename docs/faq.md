# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is Ballie?
Ballie is an all-in-one business management platform designed for Nigerian businesses. It combines accounting, inventory, CRM, POS, payroll, banking, and more in a single platform.

### Who is Ballie for?
Small and medium businesses in Nigeria that need a unified system to manage their operations.

### Is my data secure?
Yes. Each business gets its own isolated workspace (multi-tenant architecture). Data is encrypted and access is controlled through role-based permissions.

## Account & Billing

### How do I sign up?
Visit the Ballie website and click "Get Started." See our [Registration guide](/getting-started/registration) for details.

### Can I change my plan later?
Yes. Navigate to **Settings → Subscription** to upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time.

### Is there a free trial?
Yes, new accounts start with a free trial period to explore all features.

## Features

### Can I use Ballie on my phone?
Yes. The [Ballie mobile app](/mobile-app/) is available for Android and iOS with offline support.

### Does Ballie work offline?
The mobile app supports offline mode. The web app requires an internet connection.

### Can I import my existing data?
Yes. You can import customers, vendors, products, and chart of accounts via CSV upload. See [Customer Import](/crm/customers) for details.

### How do I handle Nigerian taxes (PAYE, VAT, Pension)?
Ballie has built-in [statutory compliance](/payroll/statutory) for PAYE, pension, and NHF. VAT is handled through the tax settings.

## Troubleshooting

### I forgot my password
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. A reset link will be sent to your email.

### My module is not showing in the sidebar
Check that the module is enabled in **Settings → Modules**. See [Modules](/settings/modules).

### How do I contact support?
Navigate to **Support** in the sidebar or [submit a ticket](/support/tickets).
