# Mobile Features

The Ballie mobile app provides access to essential business features on the go.

## Available Modules

- **Dashboard** — Key metrics and quick stats
- **Invoices** — Create and manage invoices
- **Customers** — View and manage customer records
- **Products** — Browse and update inventory
- **POS** — Process sales from your phone
- **Attendance** — QR code check-in/out for employees

## Offline Mode

The app works without an internet connection:
- Create invoices and sales offline
- Data syncs automatically when you reconnect
- A sync indicator shows pending changes

## Push Notifications

Receive alerts for:
- New orders and payments
- Invoice due dates
- Low stock warnings
- Approval requests
