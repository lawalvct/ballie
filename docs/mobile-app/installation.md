# Installation

Get the Ballie mobile app on your Android or iOS device.

## Android

1. Download the APK from the Ballie download page
2. Enable "Install from unknown sources" if prompted
3. Install and open the app
4. Log in with your Ballie credentials

## iOS

1. Download from the App Store (when available)
2. Install and open the app
3. Log in with your Ballie credentials

## First Login

1. Enter your **Business URL** (e.g., `yourbusiness.ballie.co`)
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password**
3. The app syncs your data automatically
