# Subscription & Billing

Manage your subscription plan, view billing history, and upgrade or downgrade as needed.

## Viewing Your Plan

Navigate to **Settings → Subscription** to see:
- Current plan and features included
- Next billing date
- Payment history

## Upgrading

1. Click **Upgrade Plan**
2. Select your new plan
3. Complete payment via Paystack
4. New features are available immediately

## Billing History

View and download invoices for all past payments.
