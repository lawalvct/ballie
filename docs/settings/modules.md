# Modules

Ballie's modular design lets you enable only the features your business needs. Disabled modules are hidden from the sidebar and won't appear in navigation.

## Managing Modules

1. Navigate to **Settings → Modules**
2. Toggle modules on or off:
   - Accounting
   - Inventory
   - CRM
   - POS
   - Payroll & HR
   - Banking
   - Projects
   - E-Commerce
   - Procurement
3. Changes take effect immediately

::: tip
You can enable modules at any time as your business grows. Your data is preserved even when a module is disabled.
:::
