# Company Settings

Manage your company's profile, branding, and business preferences.

## Accessing Company Settings

Navigate to **Settings → Company Settings**.

## Configuration Options

- **Company Name** and **Trading Name**
- **Logo** — Upload your company logo
- **Address** — Business address details
- **Contact Information** — Phone, email, website
- **Tax ID (TIN)** — Tax Identification Number
- **Financial Year** — Start month of your fiscal year
- **Currency** — Primary business currency
- **Date Format** — Display format for dates
- **Invoice Prefix** — Customize invoice numbering
