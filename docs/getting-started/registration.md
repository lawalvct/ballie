# Registration

Getting started with Ballie takes just a few minutes. Create your account, set up your business, and you're ready to go.

## Creating Your Account

1. Visit the Ballie website and click **Get Started** or **Sign Up**
2. Fill in your details:
   - **Full Name** — Your name as the account owner
   - **Email Address** — Used for login and notifications
   - **Password** — Minimum 8 characters
   - **Phone Number** — For account recovery and notifications
3. Click **Create Account**
4. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox

## Setting Up Your Business

After registration, you'll create your first business:

1. Enter your **Business Name**
2. Select your **Business Structure** (e.g., Sole Proprietorship, Limited Company)
3. Choose your **Industry/Category**
4. Set your **Currency** (defaults to NGN)
5. Click **Create Business**

## Choosing a Plan

Ballie offers multiple subscription tiers. You can start with a free trial and upgrade later.

::: tip
You can change your plan at any time from **Settings → Subscription & Billing**.
:::
