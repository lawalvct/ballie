# Onboarding Wizard

After creating your business, Ballie guides you through a 4-step onboarding wizard to configure your workspace.

## Step 1: Company Information

Enter your company details:
- **Company Name** and **Trading Name**
- **Address**, **City**, **State**
- **Phone Number** and **Email**
- **Tax Identification Number (TIN)** (optional)
- **Company Logo** (optional)

## Step 2: Preferences

Configure your business preferences:
- **Financial Year Start** — When your fiscal year begins
- **Date Format** — Choose your preferred date display format
- **Currency Settings** — Primary currency and decimal places
- **Invoice Prefix** — Customize your invoice numbering

## Step 3: Chart of Accounts

Set up your accounting foundation:
- Choose a **default chart of accounts** template or create a custom one
- Review the pre-configured account groups (Assets, Liabilities, Equity, Revenue, Expenses)
- Add any additional ledger accounts specific to your business

## Step 4: Completion

Review your settings and finalize:
- Confirm all details are correct
- Click **Complete Setup** to enter your dashboard
- Ballie creates your workspace with all enabled modules

::: tip
You can update any of these settings later from **Settings → Company Settings**.
:::
