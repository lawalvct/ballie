# Navigating the Dashboard

The Ballie dashboard is your central hub for managing your business. It provides quick access to all modules, key metrics, and recent activity.

## Dashboard Layout

The dashboard displays:
- **Revenue and expense summaries** for the current period
- **Recent invoices** and their payment status
- **Outstanding balances** from customers and vendors
- **Quick action buttons** for common tasks
- **Charts and graphs** showing business performance trends

## Sidebar Navigation

The left sidebar provides access to all modules:
- **Dashboard** — Home overview
- **Accounting** — Invoices, vouchers, expenses, payments, chart of accounts
- **Inventory** — Products, categories, stock management
- **CRM** — Customers and vendors
- **POS** — Point of sale terminal
- **Payroll & HR** — Employees, attendance, payroll processing
- **Banking** — Bank accounts and reconciliation
- **Projects** — Project management
- **E-Commerce** — Online store management
- **Procurement** — Purchase orders
- **Reports** — All report types
- **Settings** — Company, users, modules, subscription

::: tip
Modules you haven't enabled won't appear in the sidebar. Go to **Settings → Modules** to enable or disable modules.
:::

## Global Search

Use the search bar at the top of the page to quickly find:
- Customers and vendors
- Invoices and quotations
- Products
- Employees
- Any record across all modules
