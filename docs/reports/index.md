# Reports

Ballie provides comprehensive reports across all modules — financial statements, sales analytics, purchase tracking, and inventory valuation.

## Report Categories

- **[Financial Reports](/reports/financial-reports)** — Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, Trial Balance, Cash Flow
- **[Sales Reports](/reports/sales-reports)** — Sales by period, customer, and product
- **[Purchase Reports](/reports/purchase-reports)** — Purchase analytics and vendor spend
- **[Inventory Reports](/reports/inventory-reports)** — Stock valuation and movement reports

## Accessing Reports

1. Navigate to **Reports** from the sidebar
2. Select the report category
3. Configure date ranges and filters
4. Click **Generate**
5. Export to PDF or Excel as needed
