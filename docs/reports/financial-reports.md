# Financial Reports

Generate key financial statements to understand your business performance.

## Available Reports

### Profit & Loss (Income Statement)
Shows revenue minus expenses for a given period.

### Balance Sheet
Snapshot of assets, liabilities, and equity at a specific date.

### Trial Balance
Lists all ledger accounts with their debit and credit balances.

### Cash Flow Statement
Tracks cash inflows and outflows by category.

## Generating a Report

1. Navigate to **Reports → Financial**
2. Select the report type
3. Set the **Date Range**
4. Click **Generate**
5. Download as PDF or Excel
