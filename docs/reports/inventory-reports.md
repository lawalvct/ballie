# Inventory Reports

Monitor your inventory value and movement patterns.

## Available Reports

- **Stock Valuation** — Current value of all inventory
- **Stock Movement** — Tracking stock in/out over time
- **Stock Summary** — Overview of stock levels
- **Low Stock Alert** — Products below reorder threshold
- **Bin Card** — Detailed per-product stock history
