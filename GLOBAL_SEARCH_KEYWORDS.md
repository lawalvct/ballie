## Tips

- Search is **case-insensitive**
- Type at least **2 characters** to trigger search
- Results are **cached for 5 minutes** in your browser
- Use **Ctrl+K** to open search from anywhere
- Use **arrow keys** (↑↓) to navigate results, **Enter** to open
- Use **category filter pills** to narrow results
- Recent searches are saved and shown when search is empty
